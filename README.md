# Create-Sable
A pure sandbox engineering modpack focused on Create (and it's add-ons), mechanical automation, and aeronautical physics.

<hr>
![Create Sable Welcome](https://cdn.modrinth.com/data/cached_images/1cb1d6020a77a940243172ef5f3365ac7d8955b1.png)


### Welcome to Create: Sable!
A modpack that focuses on your creativity, that is build around Create. This pack is designed for casual **thinkers**, **builders**, and **inventors** who want to push the boundaries of mechanical automation, electrical automation, ~~flight~~, and survival without being tied down by quest (WIP)

<hr>
You have the tools, Take it to the Skies, Build anything that is possible, No quest, Just be creative.

## Features:


### **Aeronautical Physics:**
With Create: Aeronautics, Build fully functional airships, planes, flying builds, Thanks to the **Sable** physics engine, your builds now have physics!

### **Next-Level Automation:** 
Deeply integrated mechanical systems allow you to automate everything from resource gathering to massive industrial factories.

### **Pure Sandbox Freedom:** 
There are no quests. No forced progression, no gated content, and no hand-holding. You are entirely free to set your own goals, build at your own pace, and master the skies your way.

### **Optimized for Performance:** 
Built on Modrinth to ensure smooth frame rates and stable physics simulation, even when your massive airships are in mid-flight.
